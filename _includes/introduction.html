Shangrong Yang 杨尚蓉

I am pursuing the Ph.D. degree at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) in MePro (led by Prof. Yao Zhao), advised by Prof. Chunyu Lin. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor degree at Computer Science and Technology from BJTU.

I was born and raised in Guigang, a picturesque city with colorful culture in China.

Research Interests

My research interest lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. In particular, my work focuses on low-level vision, including: image generation/restoration/enhancement, video processing, distortion rectification, inpainting and outpainting, object detection and tracking, etc. I am also interested in vision transformer and diffsuion.
