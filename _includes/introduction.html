Shangrong Yang 杨尚蓉

I am currently a Research Fellow at the MMLab@NTU, S-Lab, and working with Prof. Chen Change Loy, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Before joining NTU, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2023 at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) in MePro, advised by Prof. Chunyu Lin. I was a visiting scholar at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPII) in Computer Graphics Department advised by Vahid Babaei (Oct. 2021 - Oct. 2022).

I was born and raised in Anqing, a picturesque city with colorful culture in China.

Research Interests

My research interest lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. In particular, my work focuses on image generation/restoration, multiple view geometry, 3D vision and point cloud, and 360° panoramic scene understanding, etc. I am also interested in reinforcement learning-based advanced manufacturing.
